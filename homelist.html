What is a BAA Home Lift?

A unique vertical moving Hydraulic device custom-built to fit the available space in your home. Each lift is designed from scratch based on your architectural layout and personal requirements.

The proven designs are rugged and incorporate the latest safety features like Rotary Limit Switches in the Electric Hoist, Limit Switches for stopping the lift at various floors, and Electronic Door Locks for maximum security.

Built-in Safety Features

Rotary Limit Switches

Integrated into the Electric Hoist for precise stopping at each floor level — eliminates over-travel.

Floor Limit Switches

Automatic stopping mechanism at each floor — smooth, reliable, and jerk-free stops every time.

Electronic Door Locks

Electronically controlled door locks prevent opening during movement — maximum passenger safety.
