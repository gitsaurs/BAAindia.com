Engineering Solutions for Unique Challenges

Special Purpose Machines (SPMs) are custom-built machines designed for a specific manufacturing process. Unlike standard machines, SPMs are engineered from scratch to solve a challenge no standard equipment can handle.

BAA's team of experienced engineers — with 30+ years of expertise in Hydraulics, Pneumatics, PLC Controls, and Mechanical Systems — brings innovation to every SPM project.
