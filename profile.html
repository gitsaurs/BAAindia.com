The Bhaskara
Legacy

Bhaskara Advanced Automation was established in 1994 by a dynamic team of Engineers, headed by Mr. H. V. Raja, in Bangalore, India. Starting from a vision to bring world-class automation to Indian industry, BAA has since grown into a group of 5 companies.

The Bhaskara Group is equipped with the latest technology and machinery for General Fabrication, Machining and Assembly of Standard and Special Purpose equipment and systems — delivering engineering solutions that are robust, reliable, and designed to last.

All our equipment carry a warranty of one year against any manufacturing defects. We offer a close-knit service network with a well-trained team of installation and maintenance engineers available 24/7.

Mr. H. V. Raja

Founder & Managing Director

Mr. H. V. Raja brings over 38 years of experience in the field of Design, Development and Manufacture, gained at some of India's most reputed companies.

He has been involved in product design and development in diverse areas such as Machine Tool Building, Material Handling, Instrumentation, and Welding Automation using Hydraulics, Pneumatics and PLC Controls.
